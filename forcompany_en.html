Advantages of Working with Nadis Nadis is a convenient advertising platform with social network elements, where in real-time, you can provide users with a full range of products, post news about discounts and promotions. Customers can view your individual account and contact you directly to place an order! Also, depending on the subscription plan you choose, the promotion of your product or service in the Nadis marketplace news feed will vary! With Nadis, you gain access to an active, real audience without bots or artificially inflated metrics — only live users who actually make purchases. Every review on the platform is an honest opinion from a real customer who made a purchase. An intuitive personal dashboard provides quick access to all functions: from analyzing statistics to managing reviews and communicating with customers.
With Nadis, you save resources, increase brand awareness, and build loyal relationships with customers — easily, transparently, and effectively.

Important Information

Please be vigilant and cautious. Due to the increasing cases of fraudulent activities, we inform you that employees of Nadis Group Limited do not call users or partners of Nadis requesting any data, including information about the balance in Nadis, order/purchase history, passport details, or bank account information. All necessary data is filled in/edited by users only in the Nadis mobile app independently.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can leave comments and ratings?
Only users who have made a purchase in your store.
Can I delegate account management?
Yes, this feature is available in the business account dashboard.
How will I receive payment for a bonus purchase?
Transfer to your bank account from Nadis within the specified timeframe.
What can I post on Nadis?
Products, services, posts, stories, banners, promotions, discounts, vacancies.
What documents are required for registration?
Constituent documents (list provided in the app) and signing the agreement.
Is content moderated?
Yes. We review posted content for compliance with platform rules.
Can I operate as an individual?
No. Only legal entities and individual entrepreneurs.
Are there paid promotion services?
Yes. Additional promotion tools are available in the personal dashboard.
